HOBBIES, INTERESTS AND GOALS

SPORTS

Sports is what I would say is my personal favourite group of hobbies. I have been participaiting in organised sports since the age of 3 or 4, and have explored many different types over the years.

Currently, my main sport is basketball. I play for both club (Tuakau Bullets) and School, and have been playing seriously for the higher end of 2 years. My secondary sports are Track (running), Badminton, Mixed Martial Arts (predominantly boxing), Volleyball, High-Jump, and Darts.

MUSIC

If anything comes close to sports, it's music. I have been interested in music since a young age and as of now can proficiently play the drums and ukulele, as well as many drum variants. Instruments I can play, but not-as-well as the others include the recorder, guitar, keyboard, and the bass guitar.

In regards to musical interests and what I enjoy listening to, my palette is vast. A few of my favourite artists include Kendrick Lamar, XXXtentacion, Brent Faiyaz and Lauryn Hill. I listen to various genres such as Hip-Hop, Rap, Classical, Jazz, Rock, RnB, etc. I enjoy thought-provoking and relatable music, as well as music that is enjoyable and catchy.

OTHER

My other interests include that of reading, watching anime/reading manga, cooking, going to the gym, singing and dancing, gaming, learning languages, and spending quality time with people.
